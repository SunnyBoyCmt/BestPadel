How the Americano System Works

Round Robin Format

Every player plays with every other player exactly once, ensuring maximum fairness

Point System

Players earn points based on their performance in each match. The player with the most points wins

Efficient Scheduling

Matches are scheduled to use all available courts optimally, minimizing waiting time

Clear Ranking

Final standings are determined by total points, with tie-breakers for equal scores
